● Photolithography, plasma etch, and film deposition tools in OSU cleanroom

● Four probe station with heated chuck

● Keysight B1500A Semiconductor Device Parameter Analyzer capable of I-V, C-V, and pulse measurement

● Keithley 4200-SCS Semiconductor Device Parameter Analyzer capable of I-V, C-V

● AFM, PFM, and MFM are available for shared research facilities
