About the Lab

Welcome to Brain-inspired Computing Lab at Oregon State University!
We aim to co-design hardware and software to realize energy-efficient intelligent systems. Our research scope spans the whole stack of electrical engineering and computer science to accomplish what requires synergy across multiple areas. Possible applications are, but not limited to, Neuromorphic Computing, Natural Computing, Physics-based Neural Network.

Please click through this site to find more information. We are always excited to meet motivated undergraduate and graduate students! Interested students are encouraged to contact us to discuss available projects.
