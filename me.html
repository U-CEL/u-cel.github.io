Welcome to the Neural Electronics Lab at Oregon State University!
We aim to co-design hardware and software to realize energy-efficient intelligent systems. Our research scope spans whole stack of electrical and computer engineering to accomplish what requires synergetic efforts across multiple areas. Possible applications are, but not limited to, Neuromorphic Computing, Probablistic Computing, Physics-based Neural Network.

Please click through this site to find more information. We are always excited to meet motivated undergraduate and graduate students! Interested students are encouraged to contact us to discuss available projects.
